Evacuation Plan Louvre Museum

Project for Mathematical Contest in Modeling (MCM) 2019

Members: Thong Vo, Mykhe Bac Nguyen, Runchang Zhou

Learn more about this contest
In this contest, there were 11262 teams representing institutions from fifteen countries/regions.

Statement of the problem

In 5 days, we had to do research, create model, and write a paper to an open-ended interdisciplinary modeling problem. Our problem is designing a evacuation model for Lourve museum which can evacuate all occupants as quickly and safely as possible.
The model needs to satisfying these conditions:

  • Taking diversity of visitors - speaking variety of languages, groups traveling together, and disabled visitors - into consideration
  • Considering when and how many additional exits might be utilized to preserve security of the museum
  • Identifying and resolving bottlenecks
  • Considering different types of potential threats

Then, we have to discuss how the Louvre would implement that model.
