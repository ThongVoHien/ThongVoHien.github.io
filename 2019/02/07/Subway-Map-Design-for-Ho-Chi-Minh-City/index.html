Problem statement

The design for Ho Chi Minh City Subway System was proposed in 2001 and will be finished in 2020. This system will resolve problems related to transportation.

The purpose of our project is, based on that design, building an optimal model of underground system, which will satisfy these conditions:

  • All the main districts in Ho Chi Minh City will have at least one route.
  • There are at most 6 routes (because there are only 6 routes in the proposed design).
  • The subway system is independent of other means of transportation.
